// chapter I — the journey

From the first stroke to the infinite

Every masterwork begins with a single stroke — a moment of intention where the brush meets the page. My journey into development started the same way: a single question, "what if I could build worlds?"

From the forges of game development to the sculptor's studio of 3D art, from the alchemist's lab of visual effects to the architect's drafting table of web development — each discipline is a different ink in my well, a different stroke in my story.

I don't just write code. I inscribe intention into every line, carve meaning into every polygon, and kindle magic in every particle. This manuscript is my testament.
